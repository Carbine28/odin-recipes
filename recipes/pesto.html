Pesto Recipe

Description

A simple-to-make pesto recipe consisting of 6 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of basil leaves
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 quarter cups of grated parmesan cheese
  • Half cup of olive oil
  • Quarter cup of Pine nuts
  • Half cup of chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Combine basil, garlic, parmesan cheese, olive oil, and nuts into food processor.
  2. Blend to a smooth paste.
  3. Add parsley if desired.
