Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

A Triple chocolate chunk cookie recipe which you can make easiliy in less than 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 Quarter cup of brown sugar
  • Half cup of unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • Half cup of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 and a half cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of chocholate chips
  • 1 bar of dark chocolate, cut into chunks
  • 3 tablespoons of white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar, white sugar, and salt into a large bowl.Beat with an electric mixer until creamy , deep brown mixture forms. Add egg and vaille, beat until mixture lightens and becomes smooth.
  2. Mix flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl. Add slowly to the wet ingredients until mostly incorporated, but some white traces of flour remain. Fold in milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate using a spatula, not the mixer. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 48 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C when ready to bake.
  4. Divide cold dough into 2 1/4-ounce portions and place on a cookie sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges start to look golden brown and crispy, 10 to 12 minutes. Do not overbake; the centers will not look fully done. Cool until cookies are set, about 30 minutes.
